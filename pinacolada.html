Pina Colada

  • 4 oz coconut cream
  • 8 oz white rum
  • 12 oz pineapple juice
  • 5 cups of ice
Mix together coconut cream and pineapple juice until creamy.
Add desired amount of white rum.
Add ice and blend.
Garnish with pineapple slice and maraschino cherry.
