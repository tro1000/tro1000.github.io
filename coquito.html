Coquito

Puerto Rican Eggnog

  • 32 oz coconut milk
  • 16 oz condensed milk
  • 1 cup Bacardi rum
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 cup of raisins
  • 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • hint of salt
Combine coconut milk, condensed milk, and water in a blender.
Add desired amount of Bacardi to mix.
Add water to thin drink to liking.
Sprinkle in cinnamon and salt.
Garnish with raisins on top.
